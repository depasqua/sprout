# Email Templates

**As a** system administrator  
**I want to** create and manage email templates  
**So that** I can send consistent, timely communications

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Templates for different time intervals (2 weeks, 4 weeks, 8 weeks, 12 weeks)
- Templates for special occasions (New Year's resolutions, Child Abuse Awareness Month, etc.)
- Templates support personalization (name, date, etc.)
- Templates can be edited and updated
